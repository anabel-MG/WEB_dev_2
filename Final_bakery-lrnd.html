Take Away


Overall reflection
Looking back at all the interviews I did for my baking journey, I realize how much I've learned from each person. From my neighbor to my aunt to my classmate, they all shared helpful tips and encouragement that shaped how I bake. Their stories and advice made me appreciate baking even more. I feel more confident to try new things, whether it's trying traditional recipes or mastering important techniques. Each interview taught me something valuable about baking. But the biggest lesson I learned is how important it is to have support from others. Their encouragement motivates me to keep baking and exploring new recipes. I'm thankful for their guidance and excited for what's next in my baking journey.
