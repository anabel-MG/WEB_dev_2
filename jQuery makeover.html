Choosing Colors

Color can breathe life into any form of media, whether it's a website, poster, or drawing. In this context, I'll showcase examples using my own artwork, focusing on four distinct color combinations or palettes to illustrate and explain how color can influence the mood of media. For these demonstrations, I've selected four color schemes: neon (or blacklight, for a more playful name :D), featuring highly saturated colors; pastel, incorporating lighter, soft hues; dark, as the name suggests, using darker tones; and natural, which embraces colors that are neither too light nor too dark—often inspired by nature.
